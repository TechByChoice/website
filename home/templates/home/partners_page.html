TBC Partners

Sponsoring Tech By Choice is a great way to recruit skilled, well-rounded, individuals from underrepresented groups to fill key positions within your organization. By sponsoring TBC, you demonstrate your commitment not only to DEI within your company, but also to the professional development of underrepresented people in the tech industry at a whole.

By becoming an early supporter of this grassroots nonprofit, you can help us provide classes, workshops, and critical resources for our members, including tickets to conferences, discounts on professional training programs, and more.

The demand for diversity within STEM fields is at an all-time high, despite the slow improvements; donating to Tech By Choice is one way you can help increase these numbers and help create an industry where members of underrepresented groups can thrive
